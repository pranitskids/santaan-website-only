# Santaan Content Team Playbook (SEO + Conversion)

This playbook ensures every Medium article can be auto-synced into Santaan website pages and rank better in search.

## 1) Mandatory structure for every blog
- One primary keyword per article (for example: `blocked fallopian tubes symptoms`).
- One clear H1 title with the primary keyword near the start.
- Opening paragraph should explain user problem in plain language.
- Use H2 sections for intent-based subtopics:
  - Symptoms
  - Causes
  - Diagnosis
  - Treatment options
  - When to consult a specialist
- Add a short FAQ section with 3-5 real patient questions.
- End with a CTA block:
  - `Book fertility assessment`
  - `Call Santaan centre`
  - `WhatsApp for guidance`

## 2) Medium publishing rules (critical for auto-sync)
- Publish from `@santaanIVF` only.
- Add 4-6 tags per article.
- Use tag `santaan-news` only for announcements/news.
- Do not use `santaan-news` for educational blogs.
- Ensure featured image is set (used as card image on santaan.in).
- Include one-sentence summary in first 2 paragraphs (becomes excerpt).

## 3) SEO writing checklist before publish
- Include city/service context naturally where relevant:
  - Bhubaneswar
  - Berhampur
  - Bangalore
- Mention one core Santaan service page in body copy with direct URL:
  - `https://santaan.in/pcos-fertility-treatment`
  - `https://santaan.in/male-infertility-clinic`
  - `https://santaan.in/thyroid-and-fertility`
  - `https://santaan.in/forgotten-fever-tubal-factor`
- Keep paragraphs short (2-4 lines).
- Add one comparison table or bullet summary when possible.
- Avoid clickbait titles; use intent + clarity.

## 4) Conversion blocks to include in every blog
Use this pattern near the end:

- `Who should act now?` (2-3 bullet points)
- `What to do next?` (consultation/assessment path)
- `Book now` link to `https://santaan.in/at-home-fertility-testing`
- `Call now` link to `tel:+919337326896`
- `WhatsApp now` link to `https://wa.me/919337326896`

## 5) Editorial cadence and topic mix
Weekly target: 2 posts
- 1 high-intent service keyword post
- 1 educational myth-busting post tied to service CTA

Monthly cluster targets:
- PCOS and ovulation
- Male fertility and sperm quality
- Thyroid and conception
- Tubal factor and infection history
- IVF planning and treatment expectations

## 6) Quality gate before handoff
An article is publish-ready only if all are true:
- Primary keyword present in title + intro + one H2.
- At least 1 Santaan internal service URL included.
- 3-5 FAQs included.
- Conversion CTA included.
- Tagging follows news vs blog rules.
